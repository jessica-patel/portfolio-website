The Glow

Topics: TouchDesigner Circuitry Arduino Fiber Optics Sensors

🛠 Tools: Arduino, Circuitry,
💻 Programming: Arduino (C/C++), TouchDesigner
🕑 Time Span: 1 month
✅ Result: Immersive and reactive installation for the public at the 2023 Digital Futures Open Show.
