The Orchestra

A movement-reactive opera singing installation using Processing, motors and 3D printed linear actuators. Based on where you are standing, 1 of 3 opera singers begin singing, allowing you to conduct the orchestra!

Topics: Computer Vision Circuitry Arduino Processing Servo Motors 3D Printing

🛠 Tools: 3D-Printed Linear Actuators, Servo Motor, Soldering, Circuitry
💻 Programming: Processing (JavaScript)
🕑 Time Span: 1 month
✅ Result: Interactive installation showcased in the 2023 Digital Futures Open Show.
