VolleyGlove

VolleyGlove is a wearable electronics glove meant to help volleyball players improve their form and promote safety. The LED neopixels indicate whether a player is evenly applying pressure from all fingers when volleying. This helps to avoid the very common problem of jamming finger joints.

Topics: Circuitry Microcontroller Arduino Ergonomics

🛠 Tools: Conductive Fabric, Soldering, Circuitry
💻 Programming: Arduino (C/C++), FLORA Microcontroller
🕑 Time Span: 1 month
✅ Result: Glove with neopixels that light up to indicate which fingertips are applying pressure.
