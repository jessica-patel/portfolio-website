Vela - A UX Case Study

This water-weather forecasting web app delivers convenience and safety to water-sport enthusiasts through visual weather reports that are easy-to-comprehend. Water-sport enthusiasts can quickly view summarized views and full-reports, which is ideal for people on the go! This case study displays my full set of skills in UX/UI such as usability testing, visual design and prototyping.

This case study was done through the CareerFoundry UX Design program, under supervision of my in-course mentor and tutor.
