About

My Story

Hi, I'm Jess and thank you for visiting my portfolio! I curate my work here as I progress as a designer. My journey began during my bachelor’s degree in physics and astronomy. While doing research on electrons and ultra-faint dwarf galaxies, I realized that I wanted to work towards something that allowed me to produce tangible results and solutions for people around me. That’s when I found user experience design. This field excited me due to how interdisciplinary and impactful it is.

I began my UX journey by achieving a certification and practicing an abundance of skills and tools used in the industry, as shown in my Vela case-study. I will be beginning my Master of Design in September 2023 to further specialize in this field and refine my skills further. I am a problem-solver and I focus on consistently achieving growth.
